3 Ways To Cope With Depression !

Written By J.R Whitfield | July 19 2022

"Depression is silent killer when ignored and can come from many things as bereavement, divorce, illness, redundancy, job or money worries. There is no single cause of depression and it can affect others in different ways from another, If not treated or handled accordingly it can raise the risk of early death on ones life. That is why I put together a short list of 3 quick and effectively proven ways that can help you deal with depression without the embarrassment of going to ask for help which many of us fear more than depression itself."

Exercise
Exercise is a scientifically proven mood booster, decreasing symptoms of both depression and anxiety. Physical activity kicks up endorphin levels, the body’s famous “feel good” chemical produced by the brain and spinal cord that produces feelings of happiness and euphoria.
Baking
Baking is beneficial for your mental health. it can take your mind off of what is going on in the world and give you something else to focus on for however long it may be, so try something simple just to give your mind a break from what is happening in the rest of the world.
Relationship
Change your circle around stay away from people who are encouraging the symptoms of what you are going through , instead of trying to ease and help you find relief in your struggles. sometimes the main factor could be the ones who we love dearly just notice if you feel lighter or heavier before/after interacting with certain individuals.
